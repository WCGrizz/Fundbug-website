How it works

Three steps to funding you didn't know existed

Tell us about your business once. We match your profile against our curated database of active programs and send you what fits.

01
Tell us about your business
Your industry, location, size, revenue, ownership, and goals become our filter. The more specific you are, the sharper your matches.
02
We scan for programs that fit your business
We match your profile against every active program in our database — grants, loans, tax credits, and corporate programs. As programs open, close, and change throughout the year, your digest stays current. Nothing reaches you that isn't worth your time.
03
Your digest arrives
On the 1st and 3rd Tuesday, a ranked digest lands in your inbox — each opportunity explained from your perspective. Why you qualify, how competitive it is, and what to keep in mind if you apply. One click tracks it in your portal. We do the research. You make the call.
What you get

Built around your business

Every part of FundBug is designed around one question: what funding programs fit this specific business, right now?

Digest on the 1st and 3rd Tuesday
Your best matches, ranked and explained, twice a month on a predictable schedule. Your top grant opportunity is always first — everything else ordered by fit and odds.
Personalized matching
Every match includes a plain-English explanation written specifically for your business — why you qualify, what to emphasize in your application, and what to watch out for.
Human-vetted programs
We search through thousands of funding programs and filter out the ones that don't apply — nonprofit-only grants, government agency programs, healthcare and science-specific funding. Every program that makes it into our database has been reviewed by a real person for accuracy, current status, and relevance to for-profit small businesses.
Competition scoring
Every program is rated Low, Medium, High, or Lottery. Your digest is sorted so your best odds come first — no wasted afternoons chasing programs that get thousands of applicants for five awards.
Eligibility confidence score
Each match in your digest shows whether you likely qualify, partially match, or need to verify a key requirement — so you know before you click which programs are worth your time.
Application prep
For any program you're pursuing, FundBug generates tailored draft answers to the standard grant application questions — business description, use of funds, expected impact — based on your profile. A head start, not a finished product. Yours to refine and submit.
My Tracker
Every program from your digests lives in your subscriber portal. Star the ones you're pursuing, update your status from Not Started to Applied to Won, and keep your pipeline in one place between digests.
Beyond grants
Grants, matching grants, SBA loans, CDFIs, tax credits, corporate programs, state loans. The full spectrum of capital — not just the headline stuff that everyone already knows about.
Continuously updated
Programs open and close throughout the year. We track those changes on an ongoing basis so your digest reflects what's actually available right now — not what was current last quarter.
