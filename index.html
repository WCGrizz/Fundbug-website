How it works

Three steps to funding you didn't know existed

Tell us about your business once. We match your profile against our curated database of active programs and send you what fits.

01
Tell us about your business
Your industry, location, size, revenue, ownership, and goals become our filter. The more specific you are, the sharper your matches.
02
We scan for programs that fit your business
We match your profile against every active program in our database — grants, loans, tax credits, and corporate programs. As programs open, close, and change throughout the year, your digest stays current. Nothing reaches you that isn't worth your time.
03
Your digest arrives
Every two weeks, a ranked digest lands in your inbox — each opportunity explained from your perspective. Why you qualify, how competitive it is, and what to keep in mind if you apply. We do the research. You make the call.
