1.What Information We Collect

Information you provide at signup

When you create a FundBug account, we collect your name, email address, and business profile information including your industry, business location, number of employees, annual revenue, years in operation, business ownership characteristics (such as woman-owned or minority-owned status), and other details you provide to improve your matching results.

Payment information

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. FundBug does not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment card details. We receive and retain transaction records including the amount charged, billing date, and last four digits of your card for account management purposes.

Usage data

We collect information about how you interact with FundBug, including digest delivery timestamps, email open and click events (through SendGrid), feedback signals such as thumbs-up and thumbs-down responses to programs, programs you track or star in My Tracker, application status updates you make, and Application Prep answers you save. This data is used to improve your match results, personalize future Application Prep drafts, and maintain the quality of our program database.

Technical data

Standard server and access logs may include your IP address, browser type, and referring URLs when you access fundbug.net. This data is used for security and operational monitoring and is not linked to your subscriber profile for marketing purposes.

2.How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and personalize your bi-monthly funding digest
  • Match your business profile against programs in our database
  • Generate AI-assisted content including match explanations, eligibility assessments, and Application Prep draft answers
  • Improve Application Prep drafts over time using answers you have previously saved
  • Power My Tracker — storing and displaying programs you are pursuing and their status
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Improve matching accuracy and the quality of our program database
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not use your information for advertising purposes. We do not sell your data. We do not use your business information to solicit you on behalf of third parties.

AI-generated content (match explanations, eligibility scores, Application Prep answers) is produced by sending relevant portions of your business profile and program data to Anthropic's Claude API. Your data is processed by Anthropic solely to generate a response and is subject to Anthropic's data use policies. We do not use your data to train Anthropic's models.

3.How We Share Your Information

We share information only with the service providers necessary to operate FundBug:

  • Supabase — database hosting for your subscriber profile and program data
  • SendGrid — email delivery for your digest and transactional emails
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Railway — backend application hosting
  • Vercel — website hosting

Each of these providers receives only the data necessary to perform their function. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or business information with any third party for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or regulatory process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of FundBug, our subscribers, or the public.

4.Data Retention

We retain your subscriber data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, we retain your account data for a reasonable period (typically 12 months) in case you choose to reactivate, after which it may be deleted or anonymized.

Payment records are retained as required for tax and accounting compliance. Feedback signals (thumbs-up/thumbs-down), Tracker data, and Application Prep saved answers are retained to maintain the accuracy of your match preferences and to improve future drafts, even after account cancellation, unless you request deletion.

5.Your Rights and Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@fundbug.net. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your subscriber portal. You may opt out of non-essential email communications (such as product updates) while remaining subscribed to service-essential emails (such as your digest and billing receipts).

Massachusetts residents have rights under Massachusetts law regarding their personal information. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

6.Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encrypted data storage and transmission. Access to subscriber data is restricted to authorized personnel. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at hello@fundbug.net.

7.Children's Privacy

FundBug is a business-to-business service intended for adults operating small businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18. If we learn that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

8.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. For material changes, we will notify active subscribers by email. Continued use of the Service after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
