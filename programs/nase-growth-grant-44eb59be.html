About this program

The National Association for the Self-Employed awards Growth Grants of up to $4,000 to help members expand their businesses, covering things like marketing, hiring, equipment, or facility upgrades. Since 2006, NASE has given out nearly $1,000,000 through the program. An active NASE membership is required to apply. Annual, Veteran, and Gold members can apply right away; monthly members need 90 days of membership first.

Official program page: nase.org ↗

Eligibility

What are the eligibility requirements?

To be eligible, the applicant must be a member in good standing at the time of application and must maintain such good standing continuously through the date on which the award is conferred.
