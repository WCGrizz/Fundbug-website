We’re the only florist left downtown — everyone else moved to the strip mall or went online. We do weddings, funerals, corporate accounts, and walk-ins. The shop itself needs work — the cooler is original equipment and I’ve been nursing it along for two years. I also want to add a small gift section to bring in foot traffic in the slower months. I’ve never applied for a grant before. Flower costs are up, the electric bill on the cooler has crept up every year, and my insurance renewed higher again in January. I want to hear about any opportunities that fit me and make sure I don’t miss anything worthwhile.”
